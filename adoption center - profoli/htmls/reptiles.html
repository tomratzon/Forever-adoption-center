Adoptable Reptiles

Spike

Spike is a spirited Bearded Dragon with a love for basking under his heat lamp and exploring his enclosure. His favorite activities include chasing crickets and enjoying a variety of fruits and vegetables. Spike's outgoing nature makes him a great pet for beginners.

Ziggy

Ziggy is a vibrant Green Iguana known for his striking appearance and playful demeanor. He requires a spacious habitat with plenty of climbing opportunities and enjoys a diet rich in leafy greens. Ziggy is best suited for an experienced owner who can provide the care he needs.

Echo

Echo is a sleek Leopard Gecko with a calm and docile temperament. He's nocturnal and prefers a warm environment with hiding spots where he can rest during the day. Echo is low-maintenance, making him an excellent choice for those new to keeping reptiles.

Atlas

Atlas is a large Sulcata Tortoise known for his gentle nature and impressive shell. He enjoys grazing outdoors and requires a diet high in fiber with minimal protein. Atlas needs a spacious yard to roam and is ideal for owners with outdoor space.

Mabel

Mabel is a sweet Russian Tortoise with a curious and active disposition. She is smaller in size, making her easier to accommodate than some larger species. Mabel thrives in an enclosure with plenty of room to explore and requires a varied diet of leafy greens and vegetables.

Shelby

Shelby is a charming Hermann’s Tortoise with a distinct personality. She enjoys basking in the sun and burrowing into cool, moist soil. Shelby is perfect for someone looking for a pet tortoise that doesn't grow too large and can be easily managed with a proper habitat.
