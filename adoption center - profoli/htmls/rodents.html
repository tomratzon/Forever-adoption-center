Adoptable Rodents

Willow

Willow is a soft, fluffy angora rabbit with a serene and gentle nature. She enjoys lounging around and is particularly fond of being petted. Willow would be perfect for a calm household where she can relax and be pampered.

Pippin

Pippin is a small, energetic lop-eared bunny with a curious disposition. Always on the move, he loves exploring his surroundings and playing with puzzle toys. Pippin is ideal for a family that can provide lots of stimulation and playtime.

Thistle

Thistle is a charming dwarf rabbit with a coat as grey as twilight. Despite his small size, he has a big personality and loves to engage with people. Thistle is great for someone looking for a lively and interactive pet.

Nibbles

Nibbles is a tiny golden hamster with an insatiable curiosity for everything. She loves running on her wheel and burrowing into cozy blankets. Nibbles would be a delightful companion for anyone, especially first-time pet owners.

Gizmo

Gizmo is a robust Syrian hamster with a shiny black coat. He's particularly good at navigating mazes and solving problems to get treats. Gizmo would thrive in a home that can challenge his intellect and keep him engaged.

Coco

Coco is a soft, gray chinchilla with an endearing love for dust baths and chewing on wooden toys. Known for her gentle hopping and quiet chirps, Coco would be perfect for someone who appreciates the quiet charm of these unique creatures.
